FAQ

Questions before the camera rolls.

How do we choose the right clients to feature?

The best testimonial usually comes from someone who had a clear problem, trusted your process and achieved a meaningful outcome. We can help you identify the strongest stories before filming.

Do clients need to memorise anything?

No. We guide the conversation with natural questions, so they can speak honestly without needing to memorise a script.

Can testimonial videos be filmed at our office?

Yes. We can film at your office, your client's location or another suitable space depending on the story and logistics.

Can the same testimonial be used in different ways?

Yes. A single testimonial can often be edited into a full website video, shorter social media clips, ad creatives and sales follow-up assets.

What if our clients are nervous on camera?

That is normal. We create a relaxed environment and guide them through the conversation so it feels natural and comfortable.

Can testimonial videos be used in ads?

Yes. Testimonial clips can be powerful for retargeting and trust-building campaigns, especially when prospects have already heard about your business.
