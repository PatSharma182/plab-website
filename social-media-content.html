FAQ

Questions people ask before filming.

How many videos can we create in one filming day?

It depends on the format and planning, but many clients use one session to create a large batch of short-form videos, often enough for weeks or months of content.

Do I need to write scripts?

No. We'll prepare talking points or write full scripts if needed, and we can even provide a teleprompter during filming. Whether you're confident on camera or it's your first time, we'll guide you through the process so it feels natural and stress-free.

What platforms are the videos made for?

We can create content for Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube Shorts, TikTok, email campaigns, websites and ads.

What if I am awkward on camera?

That is normal. We guide the conversation, coach you through delivery and structure the filming so it feels easier.

Can these videos be used for ads too?

Yes. Some short-form videos can be edited or adapted into paid ad creatives, especially for awareness, retargeting and lead generation.

Do you help with posting?

Depending on the package, we can help with content planning, scheduling support, captions and implementation strategy.
