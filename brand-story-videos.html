FAQ

Questions people ask before filming.

What is a Brand Story Video?

A Brand Story Video is a short, strategic video that explains your business, your story, your values and the reason people should trust you.

How long should it be?

Most Brand Story Videos sit around 2-3 minutes. Long enough to create connection, short enough to keep attention.

Where should I use it?

Your homepage, About page, email signature, proposals, LinkedIn, email campaigns, Meta ads and retargeting campaigns.

Do I need to write the script?

No. We guide the messaging and structure so you know what to say without sounding scripted or robotic.

What if I’m nervous on camera?

That’s normal. We coach you through the process and make the shoot feel relaxed, clear and structured.

Is this just a video or part of a bigger strategy?

The video is only one part. We help you think through how the asset can support your website, content, sales process and follow-up.
